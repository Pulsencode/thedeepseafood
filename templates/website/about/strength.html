OUR STRENGTH

The Deep Seafood Company was established in1986; starting off with a small idea to supply quality seafood to a few organizations in Abu Dhabi the capital of U.A.E. It went on to be developed into an entire fish processing facility over the years in Ajman, later onto Dubai, UAQ and Al Ain and expanded to KSA, Oman Qatar and India. We have grown by leaps and bounds, and have become extremely successful over the years in our ventures.
