HISTORY

The Deep Seafood Company was established in 1986 in Abu Dhabi, the capital city of the United Arab Emirates, to sell premium seafood to the local market. It evolved into a seafood processing facility in Ajman, then moved to Dubai, UAQ, and Al Ain before extending to KSA, Oman, Qatar, and India. We have grown significantly and achieved remarkable success in our endeavours.
