WHY

CHOOSE US?

We aspire to deliver the unique taste of the sea and focus on delivering the best seafood to our customers.
CONSISTENT QUALITY
Our products are made following international standards, and our facilities have FSSC 22000, ISO 14001, HACCP, BRC, HALAL, EU, and USFDA certifications.
RELIABLE
We've been in the industry for over three decades, supplying the market with the highest quality seafood. We have an edge due to our experience handling and processing seafood.
SEA & FARM
We provide our consumers with a diverse choice of items to meet their needs, whether they are wild-caught or farmed.
LONG-STANDING BRAND
Our company has been in business for more than 38 years, and we have professionals at every stage of the process. Our knowledge and expertise are expertly handled to provide you with the best service possible.
RESOURCEFUL
With our extensive global sourcing capabilities, we try to organise the highest-quality items following the criteria demanded by prestigious clients. In terms of local items, we have a strong procurement staff in all emirates across the UAE that works seamlessly to arrange the best quality and needed quantities for every customer.
GLOBAL FREE TRADE PLAYER
We are utilising the free trade agreement as well as Vietnam's globalisation policy to provide the most competitive price to the consumer.
